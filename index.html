About Erin

I first fell in love with HTML/CSS when I was twelve years old and I'd learned how to customize my Xanga page.
What enthralls me about programming is the mixture of code-architecture and aesthetic to create beautiful and efficient things. It compliments the way my brain works; I'm very much a right-brained and instant-gratification-oriented learner. I see web development as an intricate mathematical system and I'm looking forward to discovering new pieces to the puzzle. I always had a natural affinity towards coding and I hope to improve on the foundations I've learned so far.

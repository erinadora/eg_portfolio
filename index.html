About Erin

I first fell in love with HTML/CSS when I was twelve years old and I'd learned how to customize my Xanga page. What enthralls me about programming is the mixture of code-architecture and aesthetic to create beautiful, efficient things. I believe it compliments the way the brain works. I view web development as an balanced mathematical system that offers the freedom to be designed abstractly. I'm looking forward to discovering new pieces to the puzzle of coding and I am excited to improve on the foundations I've learned so far.
